🗝️ Dungeon Break

Escape the dungeon… if you can.

❤️❤️❤️ | 🧠 100 | 🧍 0 | No weapon | Lv.1

WASD / Arrows = Move  ·  Space = Attack  ·  Enter = Start / Advance dialogue

🧠 The Story

You awaken deep inside a cursed dungeon. Your hands are stained with blood you don't remember spilling.

Survivors beg for help — but are they truly innocent? Kill to survive. Reach the exit.

Kill enemies to restore sanity. Walk over weapons. Find the green portal.

🎮 How to Play

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — Move
  • Space — Attack (grab a weapon first!)
  • Walk over glowing icons: weapons ⚔️🪓🗡️🔱, health ❤️, sanity 🧠
  • Walk near 🧍 survivors to rescue them (+sanity)
  • Killing enemies restores sanity
  • Find the green portal ✦ to advance
  • Survive all 10 levels — your choices shape the ending
